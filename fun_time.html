Fun Time : Idiom Time


Pay through the nose: To pay an excessively high price for something; to be overcharged.
Sentence: We had to pay through the nose for tickets to the sold-out concert, but it was worth it to see our favourite band.

Pay for itself: To generate enough money or value to cover its own cost; to be worth the initial investment.
Sentence: The new energy-efficient refrigerator will pay for itself within two years through lower electricity bills.
